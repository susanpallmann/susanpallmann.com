Best Foot Forward Ad Campaign

In Washington State, the harassment of bigfoot, sasquatch, or other undiscovered subspecies is a felony punishable by fine.

This project’s goal was to take the blue law above and transform it into an advertising campaign, spanning from large displays like billboards to something as small as a mobile web site. The campaign was to be similar to programs by the Ad Council in spreading awareness about my particular issue.

The mobile prototype is below, and the desktop version can be viewed at InVision.

Conceptualization

My approach was to treat bigfoot and bigfoot harassment like a real thing. Rather than portraying a negative message like “don’t harass bigfoot,” I realized my intent would be more effective if turned into a positive light – how the viewer should interact with bigfoot. From here I came up with my slogan, “put your best foot forward,” and my design of bigfoot as a friendly, approachable character the viewer wants to help out.

Process

One of the issues I came up against in designing for the web in previous projects was keeping everything from feeling too mechanical – digital designs that utilize non-digital design elements tend to connect better with the viewer and feel more natural. This was my focus for this project, so I hand-sculpted my bigfoot character out of clay to give it that real, hand-made feeling. The stripes and color scheme were an early decision, allowing me to relate the colorful pattern of my character to a similar stripe pattern on my web design.
