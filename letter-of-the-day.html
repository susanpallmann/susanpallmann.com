Letter of the Day Poster

Print Design
Student Project

For this semester-long project, we were tasked with finding an interesting new way to design a letter of the alphabet every day of class. However, the overall poster still needed to have a cohesive look to it. I achieved this by deciding on a set, but broad color scheme at the beginning of the project, and ensuring that no matter how I created each letter, it stayed within that color scheme. I also took time to plan the order of photographic and digital works so that no two adjacent letters would look too similar in technique.

This freed me up to be as experimental as I wanted with each letter, technique ranging from drawing in Illustrator to using one of those new 3D plastic-drawing pens.
