Futura Typography Zine Series

Print Design (Series)
Student Project
Exhibited: Margaret Bridwell Art Library November 2017

I like to think of this project as my first date with Futura. Having done my previous typography project on a traditional serif typeface, I let this be a chance to explore the other side of type – modernism and sans-serifs, all with a retro futurism style to it.
