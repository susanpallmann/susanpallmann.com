2 Truths and a Lie Website

This project was primarily research-based and an introduction to web design thinking. Just to throw a fun spin on it, the website was supposed to hold 2 truths and 1 lie, and leave it up to the viewer to guess which was the lie.

1. I got shocked by lightning in a rainforest in Belize.

2. I once walked the Great Wall of China while traveling with my family.

3. I travelled to Australia last summer and pet kangaroos there.

Conceptualization

I designed this website to be a travel/lifestyle-type site, and so turned my truths and a lie into a "bucket list" page, celebrating the idea that one can live life to the fullest. Fittingly, I also took the opportunity to include what would be a page for my daily video blog, 1 Second Everyday. All the photography, with the exception of the Great Wall, my lie, was taken on my various travels in the last few years.
