Mrs Eaves Typographic Poster

Print Design
Student Project
Exhibited: Cressman Center for Visual Arts January 2018

An introduction to the world of type, the task here was to design a poster about and using a particular typeface. Hoping to venture more into the world of serifs, I elected to make Mrs Eaves by Zuzana Licko the subject of my poster. The poster was to include an informational side, detailing the typeface’s history and samples, and an experimental one to conceptualize the typeface as a whole.

Conceptualization

Mrs Eaves was an exciting choice, for me. Aside from having one of the most interesting backstories to the name, it’s incredibly diverse, complete with hundreds of ligatures and several small caps fonts. For my poster, I wanted to highlight all the variance Mrs Eaves brings to typography, as well as accent the proper, but elegant style it holds.

… and given that it was a typeface done to remember one of the first women of typography, why not throw in some nail polish?
